About

Grocery Pop is an interactive shopping list designed to make creating and checking things off your grocery list more fun.

How to use

To add an item to your list, type the name of the item in the add item field, select a category from the dropdown list (or create your own!) and press the Add bubble button. To remove an item from your list, simply tap the bubble containing that item.

Click the sort by link to sort your bubbles using various criteria.
